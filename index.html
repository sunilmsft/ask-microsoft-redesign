★ Recommended Hybrid

Option D: Best-Of Hybrid

Combines the strongest elements from A, B, and C

  • From C: Minimal one-liner greeting — clean and immediate
  • From B: Smart prompt chips — scannable, interactive capabilities
  • From A: Subtle opt-out link — non-blocking, low friction consent
  • FAQ: Inline link below greeting (always visible, from A)
Why it works: Combines the best signal-to-noise ratio. Net-new customers see a clean greeting, can scan chips OR just type, and consent is non-blocking. No wall of text, no modal, no toggle card — just a clean chat ready to go.
